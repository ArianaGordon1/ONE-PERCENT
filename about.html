ABOUT

THE ORIGIN

ONE PERCENT was formed by a collective of high-level creatives who have spent decades dedicated to craft, aesthetics, and culture. Each came to the same understanding: true appreciation requires scarcity.

In a world of overproduction and constant noise, value has been diluted. ONE PERCENT exists as a response. We believe beauty should be intentional, access should be limited, and creation should be disciplined.

This is not about status. It is about depth, restraint, and permanence. Most will not make it. Exclusivity is intentional.

Why We’re Different

We do not design for everyone.

ONE PERCENT rejects mass appeal. Every piece is intentionally limited, engineered with restraint, and released without noise.

Our Promise

Discipline over excess.

We promise timeless design, premium materials, and controlled availability. Nothing leaves our hands without intention.

The One Percent

Not a number. A mindset.

ONE PERCENT represents those who lead, refine, and endure. It is earned, never claimed.

The Next Decade

Over the next ten years, ONE PERCENT aims to build a global luxury ecosystem defined by scarcity, craftsmanship, and legacy. We are not chasing relevance. We are creating permanence.
